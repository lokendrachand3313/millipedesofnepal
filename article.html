New Record

First Record of Spirobolid Trigoniulus corallinus (Gervais, 1842) from Nepal

Order: Spirobolida | Family: Pachybolidae

Abstract: This report documents the first occurrence of the pantropical millipede Trigoniulus corallinus in Nepal. Commonly known as the rusty millipede, specimens were collected from urban forest fragments in the Kathmandu Valley. This finding suggests a widening distribution of anthropochore species into the Himalayan foothills via human-mediated transport.

Keywords: Diplopoda, Kathmandu, Anthropochore, Urban Biodiversity, New Record.

Taxonomic Update

First Record of Gonoplectus malayus lindbergi Demange, 1961 from Nepal

Order: Spirostreptida | Family: Harpagophoridae

Abstract: The subspecies Gonoplectus malayus lindbergi is recorded for the first time within the borders of Nepal. Previously known from the Eastern Himalayas (Bhutan and West Bengal), this record extends its known distribution westward into the Central Himalayan range. Taxonomic characters and habitat preferences are discussed.

Keywords: Harpagophoridae, Sino-Himalayan, Biogeography, Gonoplectus.
